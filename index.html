Prompt 1 — If You Already Have a Resume

Copy this entire prompt and paste it into ChatGPT along with your existing resume

Copy this prompt
I want to make my resume ATS-friendly and powerful for job applications. I have attached/pasted my current resume. Do not rewrite it immediately. First, read my resume carefully and ask me all the missing questions needed to make it strong, specific, and ATS-optimized. Ask questions about: 1. The exact job title I am targeting 2. The country, city, or market I am applying in 3. The industry or company type I am targeting 4. Specific companies or brands I want to apply to, if any 5. My current and previous job responsibilities 6. Revenue, sales, customer volume, inventory value, or business volume I handled 7. Team size, leadership responsibilities, hiring, training, scheduling, and supervision 8. Tools, software, POS systems, CRM systems, technical platforms, or reporting systems I used 9. KPIs, targets, achievements, improvements, or measurable results 10. Problems I solved in previous jobs 11. Promotions, awards, recognition, or special responsibilities 12. Certifications, education, and technical skills 13. Keywords that should be included based on the target job 14. Any gaps, weaknesses, or missing proof in my current resume After I answer your questions, create a complete ATS-friendly resume with: - Professional summary - Core competencies - Work experience - Achievement-based bullet points - Skills section - Education - Certifications - Location-specific and industry-specific keywords Make the resume clean, modern, single-column, ATS-friendly, and suitable for recruiters. Avoid tables, graphics, icons, heavy formatting, and unnecessary design. Make the resume sound professional, confident, and result-driven without exaggerating or lying.
What it covers
  • Target job, location and industry
  • Responsibilities in each role
  • Numbers: revenue, team size, volume
  • Tools, software and platforms used
  • KPIs, results and achievements
  • Promotions, awards and recognition
  • Certifications and education
  • Gaps or weak spots in your resume
  • Job-specific ATS keywords
ChatGPT will ask you questions first before building the resume.

Prompt 2 — If You Are Starting From Scratch

Copy this entire prompt and paste it into ChatGPT to build your resume from zero

Copy this prompt
I want to create a complete ATS-friendly resume from scratch. Do not create the resume immediately. First, ask me all the questions needed to build a powerful, job-specific resume. Ask me questions about: 1. My full name, contact number, email, LinkedIn, and location 2. The exact job title I am targeting 3. The country, city, or market I am applying in 4. The industry or company type I want to work in 5. Specific companies or brands I want to apply to, if any 6. My total years of experience 7. My current and previous job titles 8. Company names, locations, and employment dates 9. My daily responsibilities in each role 10. Revenue, sales, customer volume, inventory value, project value, or workload I handled 11. Team size, leadership responsibilities, training, hiring, scheduling, and supervision experience 12. Tools, software, POS systems, CRM systems, technical platforms, reporting tools, or machinery I used 13. KPIs, targets, achievements, improvements, or measurable results 14. Problems I solved in previous roles 15. Promotions, awards, recognition, or special responsibilities 16. Education details 17. Certifications, licenses, and training 18. Technical skills and soft skills 19. Languages known 20. Career gap details, if any 21. Visa status or relocation preference, if relevant 22. Any job description I want to target After I answer, create a complete ATS-friendly resume with: - Name and contact section - Professional title - Professional summary - Core competencies - Work experience - Achievement-based bullet points - Skills section - Education - Certifications - Languages, if useful - Location-specific and job-specific keywords Make it clean, single-column, recruiter-friendly, and ATS-compatible. Avoid tables, graphics, icons, photos, columns, and unnecessary design. Use strong action verbs and measurable achievements wherever possible. Do not exaggerate or invent fake experience. If information is missing, ask before assuming.
What it covers
  • Your full contact details
  • Target job, location and industry
  • All previous job titles and dates
  • Daily responsibilities per role
  • Numbers: revenue, team, volume
  • Tools, software and platforms
  • KPIs, results and achievements
  • Education and certifications
  • Languages, visa and career gaps
  • Target job description keywords
ChatGPT will ask everything before writing a single word.

Step-by-Step Process

Step 1

Define Your Target

Before using AI, get clear on:

  • Job title you are applying for
  • Country or city (UAE, Canada, India)
  • Industry (retail, IT, finance)
  • Level (entry, mid, senior)

Remember

A resume must match the job. Generic resumes get ignored.
Step 2

Choose Your Starting Point

Pick the right prompt based on where you are:

  • Option A: You have a resume — use Prompt 1
  • Option B: Starting from zero — use Prompt 2
Both options are completely free. No premium account needed.
Step 3

Use ChatGPT to Extract Details

Paste the prompt and your resume into ChatGPT. It will read your input, ask structured questions, and identify what is missing.

Focus on

Numbers, responsibilities, tools used, problems solved, results achieved
Step 4

Generate the Resume

After answering all questions, ChatGPT will produce your full resume. Make sure it has:

  • Measurable results
  • Job-specific keywords
  • Industry-specific language
Step 5

Format Using Claude

Copy the ChatGPT output. Paste this into Claude:

"Convert this into a clean, professional, ATS-friendly resume. Keep it simple, single-column, and recruiter-friendly. Create this as a .docx"
Step 6

Export and Use

Export your final resume in the right format:

PDF for job applications DOCX for portals
Use a clean file name:
YourName_JobTitle_Resume.pdf

Pro Tips for the Best Results

🔢

Always include numbers. Revenue managed, team size, sales targets, customer volume.

🎯

Customize for every job. One-size-fits-all resumes perform poorly in ATS systems.

🚫

Never use tables, icons, graphics, or photos. They break ATS parsing completely.

✍️

Answer every question honestly. Do not exaggerate or invent fake experience.

🔍

Pull keywords directly from the job description and mirror the language used.

📄

Single-column, clean layout with strong action verbs and measurable achievements.
